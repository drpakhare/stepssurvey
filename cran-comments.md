## Notes

This is the first submission of **stepssurvey** to CRAN.

The package provides a complete analysis pipeline for WHO STEPS
(STEPwise Approach to NCD Risk Factor Surveillance) survey data. It has
been validated against published WHO fact sheets from three countries
(Moldova 2021, Mongolia 2019, Georgia 2016).
